Recruit Training Script

Welcome to the Federal Investigation Services! Today, we'll be covering the basics of protocol, behavior, and room functionality.

Ensure you greet everyone with a smile and professionalism. Use proper language and always follow command structure.

If the Recruit Passes

Congratulate the recruit and direct them to the badge station. Make sure they understand the next steps.

If the Recruit Fails

Provide kind but clear feedback on what was missed. Encourage them to try again and offer support if needed.
